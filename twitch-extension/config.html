FantasyLeague Extension — Setup

This extension connects your stream to the Kanaliiga FantasyLeague.

For viewers

The panel below your stream lets viewers link their FantasyLeague account to receive token drops.

For broadcasters — MVP selection

MVP selection and token drops are in Stream Manager → Quick Actions.

  1. Open Stream Manager (dashboard.twitch.tv → Stream Manager)
  2. Find the FantasyLeague tile in the Quick Actions bar at the bottom
  3. Click it to open MVP selection — available during and after matches

Quick Actions are only visible while you are live or in Stream Manager. The MVP button will not appear on the channel page.

EBS URL

Set via the Twitch Extensions Configuration Service (global segment). Contact the extension developer to change it.
