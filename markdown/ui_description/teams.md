# Teams tab

Visible to everyone.

## Teams panel

Table of all teams present in ingested match data. Columns: Team (clickable → Team detail modal), Matches, Players.

## Team detail modal

Opened by clicking a team name. Shows:

- Team name.
- **Players table** — all players associated with the team, columns: Player (clickable → Player detail modal), Matches, Avg pts, Total pts.
